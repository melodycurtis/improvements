Apply to access the Learning Records Service

A training provider can use this service to:

  • register learners
  • view learners' education history and qualifications


You can apply to access this service if you are:

  • based in the UK
  • registered with the UK Register of Learning Providers


Your session will time out after 20 mins inactivity.

Start now

What you'll need

  • your organisation's UK Provider Reference Number (UKPRN)
  • your organisation's Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) number, unless exempt
  • your Companies House number, unless a school, academy or charity
  • the number of learners that your organisation will typically enrol per year
  • the person who has been nominated to manage users' access for your organisation
  • if you need access because an awarding body requires it, you will need the accreditation certificate


If you are a school, academy, further education college, sixth form or higher education provider

  • you will need your Department of Education Northern Ireland (DENI) institution reference number if you have one
  • the person signing the agreement must be the Headteacher or Principal listed on Get Information About Schools

If you are a further education college, sixth form or higher education provider

  • The person signing the agreement must be the Headteacher or Principal listed on Get Information About Schools

If you are an independent training provider or careers service

  • The person signing the agreement must be listed as a ‘person with significant control’ on Companies House

If you are a local authority

  • The person signing the agreement must have the appropriate authority to enter into a binding contract on behalf of your organisation (such as your Chief Executive Officer or a senior manager with delegated responsibility)

If you are a charity

  • If your organisation is exempt from registering with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) then you can still apply for access to the Learning Records Service
  • You will need your organisation’s charity number
  • The person signing the agreement must be listed as a Trustee on the Charity’s Commission website
